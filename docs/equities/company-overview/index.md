# Company Overview

This section covers the first screen you would usually open when getting oriented on a company in Bloomberg.

## Pages In This Section

- [DES - Security Description](security-description.md): Core company and security snapshot.
- CN - Company News: News articles on company and peers.
- DS RES - Company Research: Company research reports.
- [FA - Financial Analysis](financial-analysis.md): Company financials and ratios.
- BICO - Bloomberg Intelligence Primer: Executive summary and comprehensive analysis.

## Suggested Flow

1. Start with [DES - Security Description](security-description.md).
2. Use the profile, pricing, and company details on `DES` to decide where to research next.
