# CN - Company News

`CN` is the company-specific news page in Bloomberg. It is useful when you want a time-ordered stream of relevant articles and a quick sense of what is driving the current narrative around a company.

## Use `CN` For

- Reviewing recent news on a company
- Prioritizing important developments
- Building background before deeper research
- Reading Bloomberg's AI-generated recap of recent events

## Toolbar Options

- Search News: (NSE)
- Actions: Open additional actions for the selected article or news view.
- Custom Search: Narrow the news feed with a more targeted search.
- Translate: Translate supported article content.
- Key Themes: (NSTM)

## Search Options

- AI News Importance
- Sources
- All Dates
- Sort By

## Page Layout

### Top News

This area highlights the most important or timely stories tied to the company.

### Background and Opinion

This area collects supporting context, analysis, and commentary around the company.

### AI Summary

Bloomberg can generate an AI summary to quickly outline the most recent developments.
